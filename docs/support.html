Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?
All your trip data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to the cloud.

Can I use Travly offline?
Yes! Trip planning, adding stops, and viewing your itinerary all work offline. Weather forecasts and travel time estimates require an internet connection.

How do I share a trip?
Open a trip, tap the share icon in the toolbar, and Travly will generate a PDF you can send via Messages, Mail, AirDrop, or any other sharing method.

What are the AI features?
On supported devices, Travly can suggest stops, explore nearby places, and help plan your day using Apple's on-device intelligence. All processing happens privately on your device.
