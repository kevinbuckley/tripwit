TripWit — Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 15, 2026

TripWit is a personal travel planning app built for families and individuals. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it's handled.

Data We Do Not Collect

TripWit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. There is no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no user accounts.

  • No data is sent to us or any third party
  • No analytics or crash reporting SDKs are included
  • No advertising frameworks are used
  • No user accounts or sign-in is required

Data Stored on Your Device

All trip data (trips, itineraries, stops, bookings, and comments) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it (e.g., exporting a PDF).

Location Data

TripWit requests access to your location only when you use the "I'm Here" feature to add a stop at your current position. Location data is used solely to:

  • Determine your current position for adding stops
  • Calculate travel times between stops using Apple Maps
  • Show your trips on a map

Your location is never transmitted to any server. It is processed entirely on your device.

Photo Library

TripWit can access your photo library to match photos to trip stops based on location and time. Photos are matched and displayed entirely on your device. No photos are uploaded or shared.

Weather Data

TripWit fetches weather forecasts from the Open-Meteo API using your trip destination's coordinates. Only geographic coordinates are sent — no personal information. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source weather API. Their privacy policy is available at open-meteo.com/en/terms.

AI Features

On supported devices, TripWit can use Apple's on-device intelligence to suggest places to visit. All AI processing happens entirely on your device. No data is sent to any cloud AI service.

Children's Privacy

TripWit does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
